# Salsa Maker Xtreme

A Roblox game built around a chaotic salsa-making experience.

## Overview

Salsa Maker Xtreme is a free for all physics experience. Large vegetables fly down a ramp and players fight to grab ingredients and make the most salsa. Chaotic by design.

The core gameplay loop was finished. Players could collect ingredients, the physics worked, the systems held together. Where the project stalled was the polishing phase. Roblox's engine was a constant fight: Blender animations didn't import cleanly, and the platform kept getting in the way of the work.

The original platform choice made sense at the time. Roblox has built-in monetization and a large existing player base, lowering the barrier to making something people would actually play. But with itch.io having no barrier to entry and Steam requiring only a minimal deposit, the tradeoff stopped making sense.

The game could be ported, but the style was built for Roblox's audience specifically. Whether that flavor of game works anywhere else is an open question.
