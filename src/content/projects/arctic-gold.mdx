# Arctic Gold

A 5-player cooperative experience set in the arctic. Your team ventures out to recover gold from the seafloor while managing life support, communications, and threats from above and below.

## Overview

Arctic Gold is an early prototype. Players must coordinate to keep the diver alive and productive: surface crew manages life support and communications while the diver works the seafloor. Gold recovery, team coordination, and defense against environmental and hostile threats all happen simultaneously.

### Dredging System

The dredging system lets the diver vacuum sediment from the seafloor to expose gold. Under the hood this is a runtime landscape masking system. A custom material uses a parameter to track the cast impact location and paint a black circle at that point at runtime, effectively erasing sediment from the surface without modifying the landscape asset itself.

### Ice Cutting

A separate tool and system that allows players to cut holes through ice using a boolean subtract operation.

### Rope Simulation

The diver is tethered by up to four lines: communications, air, material hose, and vacuum hose. A custom rope simulation handles all four simultaneously and wraps correctly around objects in the environment. Performance was a primary constraint given the number of active simulations running at once.
